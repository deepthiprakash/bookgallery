HARRY POTTER

The Philosopher's Stone

Author: J K Rowling

The book is about 11 year old Harry Potter, who receives a letter saying that he is invited to attend Hogwarts, school of witchcraft and wizardry. He then learns that a powerful wizard and his minions are after the sorcerer's stone that will make this evil wizard immortal.

Chamber of Secrets

Author: J K Rowling

After a summer spent with the Dursleys, Harry is excited to return to Hogwarts. An unexpected visitor, the house elf Dobby, tries in vain to prevent Harry from returning to Hogwarts by getting him to illegally perform magic outside of the school.

Prisoner of Azkaban

Author: J K Rowling

The saga continues as Harry is faced with dementors, the soul-sucking guards of Azkaban prison that bring icy depression into the wizarding world. The dementors are sent to guard the grounds of Hogwarts upon the escape of Sirius Black, the most wanted wizard of the age.

Goblet of Fire

Author: J K Rowling

The Goblet of Fire picks Fleur Delacour from Beauxbatons, Viktor Krum from Durmstrang and Cedric Diggory from Hogwarts. It chooses Harry as a fourth champion, forcing him to compete. Many students believe Harry cheated to enter solely because he wants more fame.

Order of the Phoenix

Author: J K Rowling

It follows Harry Potter's struggles through his fifth year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, including the surreptitious return of the antagonist Lord Voldemort, O.W.L. exams, and an obstructive Ministry of Magic.

Half-Blood Prince

Author: J K Rowling

In this book, Harry Potter learns a lot about Lord Voldemort's past, and Harry Potter prepares for the final battle against his nemesis with the help of Headmaster Dumbledore. But in that time, Voldemort returns to power, and makes a plan to destroy Harry.

Deathly Hallows

Author: J K Rowling

While villainous Lord Voldemort begins taking over the Ministry of Magic, Harry, Ron and Hermione must race against time to finish Dumbledore's quest to find and destroy Voldemort's Horcruxes in order to stop the Dark Lord once and for all.

The cursed child

Author: J K Rowling

“Harry Potter and the Cursed Child” is about the journey Albus takes while growing up, and the roles he and his best friend, Scorpius (Draco Malfoy's son), play when dark forces, perhaps in league with Voldemort, once again threaten the fate of the planet.
